HEALTHCARE

Neural Diagnostics

Client: MedTech Solutions   |   Duration: 8 Months

The Challenge

Radiologists were overwhelmed with the volume of MRI scans, leading to fatigue and a 12% increase in missed micro-fractures and early-stage tumors. MedTech Solutions needed an automated triage system that could highlight high-priority scans without generating excessive false positives.

The Solution

We trained a custom Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) on a dataset of 5 million anonymized scans. By utilizing transfer learning from our pre-trained 'Vision-X' model, we achieved high accuracy with significantly less training data than exploring from scratch.
