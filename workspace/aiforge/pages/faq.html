KNOWLEDGE BASE

Answers regarding our neural architecture, data privacy, and implementation.

General Questions

What is AIForge's core technology?
We utilize a proprietary neuro-symbolic architecture that combines the reasoning capabilities of symbolic AI with the learning power of deep neural networks, resulting in more explainable and robust models.
Can I deploy models on-premise?
Yes. Our Enterprise tier allows for full air-gapped deployment on your own hardware, ensuring complete data sovereignty and compliance with strict regulations.
Do you offer fine-tuning services?
Absolutely. Our team of architects work with you to fine-tune our base models on your specific domain data, maximizing performance for your unique use cases.

Technical Specs

What is the API latency?
Our optimized inference engine typically delivers responses in under 50ms for classification tasks and <200ms for generation tasks, depending on model size and complexity.
How is data privacy handled?
We employ differential privacy techniques and never use customer data to train our foundational models without explicit opt-in consent. Your data remains yours.
What frameworks do you support?
We provide native SDKs for Python, Node.js, and Go, and our models are compatible with ONNX, PyTorch, and TensorFlow serving environments.
